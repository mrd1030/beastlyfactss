# Affiliate Product Search List

Search terms for finding and reviewing products across five species: Bearded Dragon (BD), Leopard Gecko (LG), Ball Python (BP), Crested Gecko (CG), Corn Snake (CS).

**Note on heat sources:** modern husbandry guidance (ReptiFiles, Zen Habitats) has shifted toward overhead halogen basking bulbs on a dimming thermostat over older ceramic heat emitters (CHE) and under-tank heaters (UTH). Both approaches are listed below since UTH/CHE still dominate what's actually sold in starter kits.

## Priority order — review these first

These products recur across three or more species, so reviewing them covers the most ground and maximizes internal linking between guide pages.
